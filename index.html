Here at The Women's Coaching Collective, we believe in women helping women. We believe that women can build each other up and support one another to achieve their goals. With a network of over 200 qualified business coaches across the UK, we provide both online and face to face business coaching and mentorship. Our coaches are all experienced entrepreneurs and business owners, operating within a wide variety of sectors. With our intersectional approach we make sure everyone is supported and provided with direction and clarity that matches their individual needs.

Our coaches can help with: - Feedback on idea validity
- Business plan writing
- Market research
- Cash flow forecasts
- Funding applications
- Confidence building
- Motivation
- Advice on licensing and permits
- Goal setting
- Wealth development
- And much, much more
